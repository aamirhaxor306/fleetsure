Industry Reality Check

The Rules Have Changed.

Government crackdowns and automated enforcement are reshaping fleet operations. Stay ahead or pay the price.

Live Enforcement

AI Cameras Now Issue E-Challans Instantly on Highways

ANPR cameras across Delhi, Mumbai and 12+ state highways are actively detecting expired insurance, PUC, and permits in real-time — without stopping the vehicle. The fine arrives by SMS before you reach the next toll booth.

Impact: Instant Rs 2,000–10,000 fine per violation. No warning given.
Policy

Vehicle Scrappage Policy Now Active

Commercial vehicles 15+ years old must pass mandatory automated fitness tests. Failures lead to immediate deregistration. ~10 lakh trucks at risk nationwide.

Risk: Deregistration & loss of asset
Emission

BS-VI Stage II Emission Norms

Stricter OBD-II monitoring means higher PUC rejection rates for older fleets. More frequent renewals required to stay on the road legally.

Impact: Higher frequency of PUC failures

Sources: Ministry of Road Transport & Highways, Union Budget 2025-26, SIAM India
